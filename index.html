DAY 01

Kickoff

Today marks the official start of our website design journey! As a pair, we gathered to lay the foundation for our digital presence. We defined our core aesthetic principles, including color palettes, typography hierarchy, and layout frameworks, while selecting a primary color scheme and secondary accents to ensure brand consistency across all pages. Although we haven't set up Git yet, we're planning to initialize our repository structure with dedicated branches for design assets, documentation, and iterative development. We've also scheduled weekly syncs to review progress and adjust our approach, ensuring we build a cohesive, scalable design system from day one.

DAY 02

A busy day

Today, I focused on completing my exercises in networking and other subjects that need to be passed. While working on my networking laboratory exercises, I had an online meet with friends to collaborate and help each other. It was great to have their support and insights, especially when tackling challenging concepts. This teamwork approach made the learning process more enjoyable and effective, ensuring I could review and understand the material better. I'm looking forward to continuing this collaborative effort to stay on track with all my subjects.

DAY 03

Productive Day

Today was a normal day focused on reviewing for the supposed quiz in our subject. I spent most of my time going over the material to ensure I was well-prepared. After finishing my review, I also took care of some household chores, including washing clothes and dishes. Once everything was done, I took a short rest to recharge, as I needed to meet with my mom and run some errands later. It was a productive day, balancing study and personal tasks effectively.

DAY 04

Daily Balance

Today started with waking up early to get ready for school. When I arrived, I waited for my friends since they said they were on their way. Once they arrived, we went to the faculty to choose our topics for one of our subjects. Our first subject has a recitation every after each discussion, which I’m okay with since I managed to answer some of the questions. The day was tiring, especially since I also need to do chores when I get back home. Despite the exhaustion, I feel like I accomplished a lot today.

DAY 05

Art

Today was a nice balance of getting things done and letting my creativity flow! After finishing up my tasks for a few subjects, I finally had some time to sit down and draw. It’s been a while since I just relaxed with my art supplies, and it felt so good to lose myself in colors and shapes. I started a small sketchbook just for fun, and I’m already loving how it’s turning out. Sometimes, it’s easy to get caught up in deadlines, but taking a moment to create something just for myself is such a nice reset. Plus, it’s a great way to unwind after a busy day.

DAY 06

Meaningful Day

Today started with a simple yet essential task: washing my clothes. There’s something oddly satisfying about folding freshly laundered items, knowing they’ll be ready for tomorrow. After that, I headed to the kitchen to cook a meal for us to share. Food has a way of bringing people together, and today was no exception. We sat down, ate, and had a nice talk—just enjoying each other’s company and sharing stories from our day. Besides the cooking, I also managed to finish some school tasks. It felt good to check things off my to-do list, even if they were small. Sometimes, those little victories make a big difference in how we feel about the day. Overall, it was a day filled with small joys—from the warmth of a shared meal to the quiet satisfaction of getting things done. It’s moments like these that remind me how important it is to appreciate the simple things in life.
